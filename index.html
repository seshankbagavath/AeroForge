How AeroForge works

AeroForge designs an aircraft wing entirely in your browser. Adjust parameters in the Controls panel and every result updates live. Move through the pipeline in order:

  1. Geometry — pick a NACA airfoil and set span, chord, sweep & twist.
  2. Aerodynamics — see lift, drag and pressure at your operating point.
  3. Structure — check that the wing is strong enough under load.
  4. Optimizer — let a genetic algorithm search for the best wing.

Glossary

L/D
Lift-to-drag ratio — higher means more efficient flight.
CL / CD
Dimensionless lift & drag coefficients.
Cp
Pressure coefficient across the airfoil surface.
α (alpha)
Angle of attack — the wing's tilt into the airflow.
MAC
Mean aerodynamic chord — the representative wing chord.
Washout
Twist that lowers the tip's angle for safer stall behavior.
Safety factor
Yield strength ÷ peak stress. Target ≥ 1.5.

⚠️ A reduced-order educational model — great for intuition, not certified CFD.
