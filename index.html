What it does — in five steps

1 · Shape the wing

Adjust span, width, sweep and the airfoil profile with simple sliders. The wing rebuilds in 3D as you move them.

2 · Simulate the air

A physics model estimates how much lift the wing makes, how much drag it suffers, and where pressure builds across the surface.

3 · Check the strength

It treats the wing as a beam, calculates the bending stress at every point, and tells you if it would survive flight loads.

4 · Evolve the best

A genetic algorithm breeds hundreds of wing designs across generations, keeping the ones that fly efficiently and safely.

5 · Read the results

A clean dashboard shows efficiency, lift, drag and safety at a glance — with charts you can actually interpret.
